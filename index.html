 (Submitted) Robust Online Calibration for UWB-Aided Visual-Inertial Navigation with Bias Correction
Yizhi Zhou*, Jie Xu, Jiawei Xia, Zechen Hu, Xuan Wang
IROS, 2025 *
project code /

This paper proposes a robust online calibration method for UWB anchors in Visual-Inertial Navigation Systems (VINS). It overcomes issues with existing methods, such as reliance on accurate robot localization and sensitivity to initial anchor positions. Based on MSCKF, our approach uses stochastic optimization for reliable initialization and a Schmidt Kalman Filter for online refinement. Simulations and real-world tests show improved accuracy and robustness.

Train Human robot to walk using the open-source PPO algorithm based on Genesis
Jiawei Xia*

Based on Genesis, design and implement a RL robot control framework based on PPO from rsl_rl package, integrating multiple joint indices precisely into the framework based on the URDF model. Develop several custom reward functions, such as joint position reward, foot contact and gait matching reward, foot air-time reward, and action smoothness reward. Incorporate a fall detection mechanism during the training process to enhance training efficiency.
